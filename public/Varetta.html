While surfing the internet, I stumbled on a promotion offering an opportunity to start a business selling Herbalife products. The proposition was interesting and I could work from home, which made the idea more intriguing. I decided to purchase some of the products before signing up to offer Herbalife products to others. My family and I loved the results. The smoothie was our favorite. Everyone started dropping weight and felt energetic. Friends started asking us how we were losing weight. I explained my plans and they were very supportive. They urged me to sign up, so they could place their orders with me. That is what lead me to become a Herbalife Distributor.
